How it Works!

SafeMedia allows you to upload presentations and questionnaires , which your employees can answer based on the presentations. SafeMedia can then provide reports based on these answered questionnaires.

Account Set Up

Set up an Account with SafeMedia By simply entering company Information

Upload Presentations and Create Questionaires

Upload all powerpoint presentations to your account

Use our handy tool for setting up questionaires based on your presentations

Use on a Touch Screen Display

Download our Chrome compatible application and simply login and display all presentations and questionaires

Then just have your employees download the safemedia app and use the camera to scan the generated QR code to answer questions that are displayed on the screen with their phones
